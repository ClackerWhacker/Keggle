Mission

We love beer. We love pubs. Yet we can’t always go to our favourite establishment for a drink.

In 2020, the beer selection is better than ever. Yet cans and bottles don’t beat draught beer.

Some great home beer dispensers exist, but they only have mass-market beers on offer. Some great craft beer mini-kegs exist, but they only keep for 2-3 days.

We are democratising draught beer by building an innovative beer dispenser alongside a keg marketplace for craft beer. Now anyone can drink pub quality beer whenever they want, wherever they want.

No more mass-market beers. No more heavy kegs. No more bottles and cans.

Want some #beertech to be proud of? Want your favourite craft beer on tap? Want kegs that last 30 days?
