About

We love beer. We love pubs. Yet, we can’t always go to our favourite establishment for a drink.

Following the craft revolution, the beer selection is better than ever. Yet cans and bottles don’t beat a fresh pint.

There are some great home beer dispensers. Yet, they are usually owned by macro breweries only offering their mass-market beers.

Some independent breweries offer mini-kegs with nozzles for taps. Yet the beer only lasts about 2-3 days.

No more mass-market beers. No more heavy kegs. No more bottles and cans.
